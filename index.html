First Up: Quick History

Management Professional turned Web Developer

My education in Innovation Management (Bcomm Hons), provided tools to develop competitive advantage through leveraging unique business strengths and resources. Some examples have been:

  • creating a progressive business model for a SE company
  • developing a marketing framework to beat outdated cold-calling
  • building a production line for a unique engineering product, the first of its kind in SA.

In 2019 I completed an MBA to substantiate my skills as a leader, and joined a Geology Consultancy as Director. In this role I found the freedom to explore new things and quickly gravitated towards coding.

Being able to create web apps from start to finish is something that takes all my education and experience, and gives it form. I find this work challenging, rewarding, and more importantly, fulfilling.
